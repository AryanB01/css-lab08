Chupa chups are one of the world's largest manufacturers and distributors of confectionery and chewing gum. They create imaginative products and brands that are enjoyed in over 150 countries. One of the unique features of Chupa Chups is its long-lasting flavor. Unlike some lollipops that lose their flavor quickly, Chupa Chups are designed to keep their flavor until the very end. This makes them a popular choice for people who like to savor their candy slowly.
